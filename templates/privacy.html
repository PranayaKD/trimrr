Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Trimrr, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password when you register.
  • Link Data: Original URLs you shorten and custom aliases you create.
  • Analytics Data: IP addresses, browser type, device type, and referrer URLs of visitors who click your shortened links.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform, including pages visited and features used.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our URL shortening and analytics services.
  • To generate click statistics, device reports, and geographic insights for your links.
  • To improve, personalize, and expand our platform.
  • To communicate with you about your account and updates.
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse of the platform.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. All API communications are secured with JWT tokens. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Trimrr uses essential cookies to maintain your login session. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. Analytics tracking is limited to click-level data on shortened links to provide you with performance insights.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your personal data at any time.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and all associated link data.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
